Head of Communications

You Know

- Public trust is critical to the company's reputation.
- Journalists and customers may learn about the incident before official statements are released.
- Social media can rapidly amplify misinformation.

You Do Not Know

- Full scope of the attack.
- Whether customer information has been stolen.
- Exact recovery timelines.

Responsibilities

- Manage internal and external communications.
- Coordinate messaging with executives and legal counsel.
- Monitor social-media and press activity.
- Prepare customer-facing statements.

Key Decisions

- Issue a holding statement or remain silent?
- Respond to media inquiries immediately?
- Notify customers before technical confirmation?
- Address rumors circulating on social media?

Success Criteria

- Maintain stakeholder confidence.
- Deliver accurate and timely communications.
- Prevent reputational damage caused by misinformation.
