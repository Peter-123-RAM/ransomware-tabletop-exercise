General Counsel (GC)

You Know

- Data-breach laws may require notification to regulators and affected individuals.
- Contracts with customers and suppliers may contain security obligations.
- Decisions made during the incident may have legal consequences.

You Do Not Know

- Whether customer data was actually stolen.
- Whether payment information was exposed.
- Which regulatory reporting thresholds have been triggered.

Responsibilities

- Provide legal guidance.
- Assess regulatory obligations.
- Preserve evidence and documentation.
- Coordinate with external legal counsel if required.

Key Decisions

- Notify regulators now or wait for confirmation?
- Engage external breach counsel?
- Approve public statements before release?
- Recommend negotiation or non-negotiation with attackers?

Success Criteria

- Maintain legal compliance.
- Reduce regulatory exposure.
- Protect the organization during investigations and litigation.
