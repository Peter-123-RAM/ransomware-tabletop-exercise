Chief Operating Officer (COO)

You Know

- Daily store operations depend on inventory systems, POS systems, and supplier communications.
- Store managers are reporting difficulties accessing inventory records.
- Several retail locations may be unable to process transactions normally.

You Do Not Know

- Whether all stores are affected or only some locations.
- How long operational disruption will last.
- Whether backup systems remain functional.

Responsibilities

- Maintain business operations where possible.
- Coordinate manual workarounds for stores.
- Assess inventory and supply-chain impact.
- Advise leadership on operational risks.

Key Decisions

- Keep stores open or temporarily close affected locations?
- Switch to manual sales and inventory processes?
- Prioritize restoration of inventory or customer systems?
- Communicate operational guidance to store managers?

Success Criteria

- Minimize business disruption.
- Protect customer experience.
- Maintain continuity of essential retail operations.
