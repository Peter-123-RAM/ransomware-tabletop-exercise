Chief Financial Officer (CFO)

You Know

- Revenue losses increase every hour systems remain unavailable.
- The company maintains cyber-insurance coverage.
- The attack may create legal, recovery, and consulting costs.

You Do Not Know

- Total financial impact of the incident.
- Whether cyber-insurance will cover all losses.
- Whether stolen data can be recovered without paying a ransom.

Responsibilities

- Assess financial exposure.
- Engage cyber-insurance providers.
- Track incident-related expenses.
- Advise executives on ransom-payment considerations.

Key Decisions

- Notify the cyber-insurance carrier?
- Approve emergency incident-response spending?
- Consider ransom negotiation if recovery is delayed?
- Allocate funds for customer notification and remediation?

Success Criteria

- Limit financial losses.
- Support business recovery.
- Ensure financial decisions align with legal and regulatory requirements.
