Opening Script

Welcome everyone.

Today we will conduct a ransomware tabletop exercise.

This is a discussion-based exercise designed to evaluate decision-making under pressure.

There are no trick questions and no perfect answers.

Your objective is to protect customers, maintain operations, and minimize business impact.

Please respond as you would during a real incident.

The exercise lasts approximately 90 minutes.

Let's begin.

Closing Script

Thank you for participating.

Today's exercise highlighted strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for improvement.

The purpose was not to achieve perfect decisions but to identify areas requiring additional preparation.

All observations should be documented and converted into action items.

The exercise is now complete.
